The Fit

Who keeps a storyteller at the table

Residence is for organizations that have stopped treating story as a project and started treating it as infrastructure.

The organization with big moments coming

Raises, launches, transitions, the keynote that matters. When the calendar holds moments where the story has to be right, a standing partner beats a scramble every time.

The leadership that wants the work to compound

One engagement improves a story. A residence improves the people who tell it. Over seasons, your leaders stop borrowing the language and start authoring it themselves.

The Shape

The rhythm of a residence

A residence is not a fifth kind of work. It is the other four pillars of the Narrative Stack, run continuously instead of episodically.

The recurring read

A Coherence read on a regular cycle, so we always know where the story stands before the year asks anything of it. The trajectory becomes the answer when your board asks what this work returns.

The moments

Resonance work around launches and the rooms that matter. Legibility work ahead of board meetings, raises, and the external moments where the story faces strangers.

The seasons

Conviction work through leadership transitions and the harder passages, where the question is not what the organization says but what its leaders still believe.

The Outcome

What a residence builds

Narrative as a function rather than an event. Leaders who sound like themselves in every room. Artifacts that stay aligned without a rescue mission before each big moment. And a partner who knows your story well enough to notice it drifting before your audience does.
