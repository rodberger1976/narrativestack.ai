What it is

A workshop you can hold in your hands

Think of The Narrative Edge as the map and Your Narrative Edge as the boots. The book tells you why your story matters. The Field Guide puts you to work on yours.

Inside, you move through the same architecture I use with leaders, founders, and teams. The EDGE Method shows you where you are. The Five Rs show you what to do at each step. The Four Quadrants show you how to look at any story from every angle that matters.

And underneath all of it, you build the four things every well-told story needs.

Four pillars. One story that finally holds.

By the time you close this Guide, your story will do four things it may not be doing yet.

Coherence

It holds together across every room you walk into. The through-line stays the through-line.

Resonance

It lands. It does not just inform people. It moves them.

Conviction

You believe it, because you authored it. You did not borrow it from a title or a credential.

Legibility

People who have never met you can read it. The story travels on its own.
